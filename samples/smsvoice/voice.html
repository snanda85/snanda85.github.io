Voice and Voice Messages

Services

  • Outbound voice message is a mode of tele-communication system that enables business users to send voice messages from an application to various mobile numbers.
  • Missed Call service is a service available on the voice platform wherein mobile phone users can call a particular number to register their opinion/interest on a particular matter. It is particularly useful in voting scenarios and opinion collection. It is extremely popular in the masses since a missed call is not chargable and the caller does not pay anything.
  • Automated Receptionist as the name suggests is a service to automate the incoming calls which a receptionist would normally take. It comes with features like call forwarding, Holiday and office time mapping.
  • Toll Free numbers are utilized by organizations who do not wish to charge their customers for having called them.

Benefits of Voice Messages

  1. Outbound Voice Services have a greater reach that SMS since voice call scan also be made to landline numbers unlike SMS. This increased reach opens up fresh avenue of advertising which were not available on SMS.
  2. Outbound Voice Messages can be made creative by the use of multiple languages and music. Moreover to listen to a voice message one need to be literate (as in the case of SMS).
