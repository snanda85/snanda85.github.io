SMS Marketing

SMS is a mode of tele-communication system that enables cellular phone users to send and receive text messages from one mobile number to another. For business communication purposes the SMS communication is initiated from an application to be terminated on targeted mobile numbers.

Benefits of SMS

  1. The mobile to mobile messaging system (P2P) has capacity limitations and if a business wishes to push SMS communication to its customers in large quantities on a regular basis, P2P just does not deliver.
  2. Application to Peer messages are economical, quick & effective and end up saving time & effort for the business owner which in due time translates into monetary banefits.
  3. It is a marketing tool that is specific, personalized, easy and non-disturbing. It does not invade the privacy of the recipient as it can be stored and read at leisure. The cost of pushing SMS to targeted segments is a fraction of the cost involved in traditional advertising efforts.
